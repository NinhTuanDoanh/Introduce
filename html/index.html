About me


Who i am


My name is Tran Si Phu, a graphic
designer and video editor with more than
5 years experience. I have worked in
many different types of design fields
from 2013 in several companies such as
Digital Works, YAN TV, TCHEM, Ariska,
Hoang Gia Pearl, VCG, BankMart,
Idocean.


I own my design label which named
“Sifu”. Its publishing my works as a
partner or independently. I am very eager
and enthusiastic about the opportunity
of working and learning.


I have worked with a number of clients,
both independently and as a full-time
staff. Currently was as Senior Designer
at Viet A Corp


The story



I began in 2016. After years working as a
full-time designer, i realized that it was
near impossible for my cousin Thy to
create her brand’s logo, a package
design for her new milk tea product and
a website for her to sell. Traditional web
hosting services were simply too
complicated, time consuming, bad
design and expensive to manage.


I created the Sifu with the customer’s
perspective in mind. I wanted to offer a
service that help people like Thy can
have a brand for their own.


Today, i am proud to empower
individuals and small business owners.
Everyone deserves a brand, and i am
excited to see what we can create
together.
